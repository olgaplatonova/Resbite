We are on a mission to repair the individual and societal damage caused by these harmful technologies and create ethically designed social technology that makes a positive impact on the world.

Resbite will bring communities together in a real way, enabling people to feel healthier and happier whilst rediscovering their authentic sense of self and the world around them.

App features

  • Location suggestions

    We help you to find locations for activities for the times when you know what to do but don’t know where to go.

  • Photo collections

    Collate photos taken by all attendees during your resbite into a central repository for download.

  • Resbite chat

    Choose to chat with either your resbite’s invitee list or just those that have confirmed attendance.

  • Flexible invitations

    Instead of a time-specific resbite, you can opt for a connection-first resbite which creates a poll to find a time that all invitees can attend.
